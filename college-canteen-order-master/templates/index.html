CollegeDine: A website for the college canteen

Our college has a lot of students, and correspondingly a lot of rush in the canteen. As a result, many of the students are left waiting to recieve their orders for a major part of the break, leaving them with no time to complete it.

With our website, you can order your food in advance, thus saving the time to come down to the canteen and placing your order. Two emails are sent to your registered Email ID; one when you place your order, and another when it is prepared and ready to be collected.
